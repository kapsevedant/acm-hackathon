About College

St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur is one of the premier private institutions providing engineering courses in Nagpur. It was founded on the belief of proving quality education. It has completed 18+ successful year of providing quality education to the students. Student from all over the state come here to study and bring out the best in them.We are pleased to inform you that our college is premier Engineering Institute, rendering quality technical education in Central India. It is run by the Nagpur Pallotine Society, part of the International Pallottine Society having its presence in 52 countries . The college is affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Maharashtra and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college has a strength ofover 2000 students from different parts of the country; is proactive in the areas of innovation & amp; research, hosting several events to encourage students. We are actively associated with e Yantra competitions, IIT Bombay and in 2016 have set up the eYantra lab under e LSI, a collaborative initiative of IIT Bombay and MHRD.
